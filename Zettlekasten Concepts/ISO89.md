# ISO89

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> ISO89 is a rule that gives us two ways of blaming people for bad security.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **ISO89 provides two definitions for how we can find out who lets threats occur in information systems.**
- Nonrepudiation (An information system can verify a user's actions and trace faults).
- Authentication (Verify user/sender identity in an information system).
