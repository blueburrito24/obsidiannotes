# Threats

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Threats are things that can mess up the stuff you like. If your bedroom door's lock is broken, a threat is that someone could go into your room when you're not there and take your books.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Threats are scenarios where loss and harm can occur to an asset.**
