# Classes of Vulnerabilities

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> So we know what vulnerabilities are. We also need to know that there are different types of vulnerabilities. For example, different vulnerabilities of your bedroom could be; your bedroom door lock being broken, your bed being a bit flimsy, your sibling swapping out your books for theirs, someone taking your books.
