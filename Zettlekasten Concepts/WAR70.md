# WAR70

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> WAR70 is a big drawing of various dangers in a computer system.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Willis Ware's WAR70 is an outline on various sources of threats in a computer system, ranging from human and non-human to hardware and software.**
- Although outdated now, with elements such as "stand-alone utility programs", Willis Ware outlined various crucial elements of [[Threats]]; threats can come from malicious employees, software confidentiality breaches, hardware failure and other elements of a computer system.
