# Integrity

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Let's say you have a drawing you made, or you wrote something. You only want you or maybe a teacher to be able to change anything on it, not any of your friends or even anyone you don't like! That's basically integrity.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Integrity is the status of the asset.**
- Nobody but the authorised parties should be able to change the asset.
- Integrity involves the asset being precise, accurate, consistent, meaningful and useable.
