# Attacks

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> If "threats" means when something bad can happen, "attacks" refers to the bad thing happening.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Attacks are explicit examples of malicious harm.**
- Attacks can be random; affecting one person and then moving onto another victim, or directed; attacking a network of computers.
- We should always try to focus on precautions however, not identifying active attacks.
- There are various [[Types of Attackers]] that can perform these attacks.
