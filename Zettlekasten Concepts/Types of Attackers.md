# Types of Attackers

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> There are all sorts of people who can be dangerous.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **There are various types of attackers and attackers are not limited to a preset of characteristics.**
- Malicious attack activity often involves MOM - Method, Opportunity, Motive. 
- There are many people online who download premade hacker/malicious packages, which are referred to as "script kiddies".
- No single attack pattern will match all attackers.
- Attackers across the world often collaborate in groups, a form of organised cyber crime, for financial gain. This has evolved into criminals enlisting hackers for things like fraud, extortion, money laundering, drug trafficking and various other network-involved crimes.
- There are also various forms of Computer Terrorism, such as:
	- Terrorism with computers as targets, such as DDOS attacks.
	- Terrorism with computers as methods, such as malware.
	- Terrorism with computers as enablers, such as co-ordinating terrorist attacks.
	- Terrorism with computers as enhancers, such as producing propaganda online to push an agenda.
