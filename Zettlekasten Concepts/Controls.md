# Controls

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Controls are what we do to make sure something we like doesn't get messed up.
> If your bedroom door's lock is broken, and you don't want someone to come in unannounced, you could put up a spare chair against the door as a "control" to make sure your door is secure.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Controls are counter-measures against vulnerabilities of an asset's security.**
