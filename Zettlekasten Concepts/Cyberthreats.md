# Cyberthreats

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Cyberthreats are dangers to multiple computers.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Security threats to a network of devices.**
- "Computer security" refers to the security of individual devices, whereas "cyber security" refers to connected/networked devices.
- Cyberthreats are threats to connected/networked devices.
