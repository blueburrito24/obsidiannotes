# Availability

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Availability means whether we can use something or not. If someone's in the bathroom, the bathroom isn't available to you. If you lock your bedroom door, your bedroom isn't available to anyone but you.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Availability refers to whether authorised parties can access an asset or not.**
