# Advanced Persistent Threats

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> If "threats" means dangers to how we keep things safe, advanced persistent threats is like a bully trying to get your lunch money everyday.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Advanced Persistent Threats refers to active harm performed continuously on a system.**
- It can often be tied to "economic espionage" - third parties tied to or affiliated with foreign governments stealing information to gain an economic advantage.
- Can involve targeted techniques like Spear-Phishing.
- It's important to note that the active harm carried out by an attacker is relative to the hacker's ability to organise and manage malicious activity.
