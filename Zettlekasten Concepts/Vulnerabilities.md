# Vulnerabilities

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Vulnerabilities are parts of something that someone can take advantage of. 
> For example if your bedroom door's lock isn't working, someone can go into your room unannounced when you're in the middle of something.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Vulnerabilities are weak spots in the security of an asset.**
