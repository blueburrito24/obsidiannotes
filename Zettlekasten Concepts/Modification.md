# Modification

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Modification is when someone changes something you have. Like if you're eating dinner and you made chicken and rice, and someone suddenly adds a drop of water in your chicken and rice. They modified your meal! And made it worse!
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Modificiation is when someone without authorisation modifies our asset.**
