# Fabrication

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Fabrication is like if someone took your book and replaced it with a similar looking book to make you think you didn't lose your actual book.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Fabrication is when someone replaces our asset or part of the asset with a counterfeit or replica.**
