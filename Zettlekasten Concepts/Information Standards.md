# Information Standards

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Information Standards are rules for how we approach Information Security - how we keep things safe.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Information Standards are definitions provided by third parties for enforcing Information Security.**
- Groups like the International Standard Organisation and US Department of Defense often provide Information Standards for which we follow when enforcing Information Security. Please see [[MOC_CS2_ISO]], [[MOC_CS2_DOD]] and [[MOC_CS2_WAR]] for explicit standards.
