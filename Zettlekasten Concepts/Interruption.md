# Interruption

## 1. Simple Explanation (Feynman Technique)
> Interruption is when someone breaks up something we have. Let's say you have a cool pencil case that you just got, and your classmate tugs on it to see it and it breaks! That was an interruption.
---
## 2. Technical Details
- **Interruption is a loss or harm made to our asset. If an asset is destroyed or damaged.**
