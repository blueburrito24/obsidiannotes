GOUKI notes; 
lk, mk, mp are great connectors for supers,
6.mp is kara input and good for avoiding grabs,

gouki's throw kara or not is great with his quick dash,

214.lk (light tatsu) is negative so do not spam,

236.p (air hadouken) is great for zoning and pressure such as after doing a sweep or while retreating etc,

626.k.p (demon flip punch) is good for getting priority on things like dp,
626.k (demon flip) is generally a good tool for mixup and oki, by extension it can be used as a combo connector,
626.hk lp.lk (demon flip grab) can be used to whiff and mix up an opponent on knockdown

624.p (red fireball) is a great pressure tool,

a.214.k (air tatsu) is a great air tool that puts the opponent into a juggle state (meaning you can connect with light tatsu or dp),
a.214.k (air tatsu) can be cancelled into sa1
626.p (dp) can be cancelled into sa1
sa1 in corner can be connected with 626.p (dp) into another sa1

214.mk (med tatsu) can connect with dp if done quickly,

raging demon is good as a punishing and mixup tool,
can be comboed into with far.hk
kara raging demon (6.mp lp lp 6 lk hp) is also great

gouki is low-health, high-stun so it's good to be on the defensive sometimes (it can be highly rewarding),
his most core tools are his air hadoukens, fierce tatsus and grabs! sort of like sean

GOUKI combos to practice:

2.lk 2.lp 2.lk (basic light combo)
2.lk 214.lk 626.hp (bread and butter)
a.214.lk 214.lk 626.hp (air tatsu into bread and butter)
a.mk hp 626.hp (air kick confirm into dp)

6.mp 2.mk 214.lk (grab punish combo with connector)

214.lk 214.hk (corner carry using light and fierce tatsu)

hp 214.lk hp 626.lk (hp tatsu hp demon flip)
HARD COMBO HIGH STUN; hp 214.lk hp 626.lk.lk hp 214.lk 626.hp (hp tatsu hp demon flip divekick hp tatsu dp)
